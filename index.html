Discover Nepal

🏙️ Kathmandu Valley is rich in culture, temples, and UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

🌄 The Annapurna Circuit is one of the most famous trekking routes in the world.

🔥 The festival of Dashain is the biggest and most important in Nepal.

🧘 Nepal is popular for yoga, meditation, and spiritual tourism.

🍛 A common traditional meal is Dal Bhat (rice, lentils, and curry).

🎵 Nepali music and dance vary across different regions and cultures.

📸 Nepal is a top destination for trekking, hiking, and mountain photography.

🐅 Nepal is home to the endangered Bengal tiger.

🦏 One‑horned rhinos live in Chitwan National Park.

🗣️ Nepal is incredibly diverse, with over 120 different languages spoken across the country.
